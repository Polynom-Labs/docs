## Before sending a support request

Include only the information needed for initial triage:

- organization and application name;
- affected Arcane product and environment;
- UTC date and time;
- network, contract, case, report, job, or public transaction identifier, where
  applicable;
- exact error message and last successful workflow stage;
- operational impact, affected users, and whether the issue is reproducible.

<Warning>
  Do not send passwords, access tokens, recovery phrases, private keys, wallet
  signatures used to derive private state, local note data, proof witnesses,
  private audit decoding material, or unredacted customer data by email. Agree
  a secure transfer method with Arcane support before sending additional
  evidence.
</Warning>
