The Arcane Compliance Platform is organized around an organization workspace. The workspace defines the client account, its members, and the applications operated under it.

Before the organization can use operational platform features, it must be verified and activated by a platform administrator.

<Info>
  You do not need to assemble a team before getting started. The person who creates the organization becomes its first member and can operate it as the sole participant.
</Info>

## Setup flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register your account">
    Select **Register organization** and provide your name, work email address, and organization name.

    The account used for registration becomes the initial owner of the organization.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create the organization">
    Complete the organization profile. The organization becomes the administrative boundary for member access and application management.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add members, if required">
    Add the people who need access and assign responsibilities appropriate to their work.

    This step is optional. An organization with one member can continue through activation and use the platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the organization for review">
    Send the organization registration request to the platform administrator. The organization enters the `Under Review` state while its details are being verified.

    Operational access remains unavailable during this review, but you can continue preparing the organization and its applications.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete verification">
    The platform administrator reviews the organization and confirms that it can access the Compliance Platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate the organization">
    After approval, the organization moves to the `Active` state. Its authorized members can then configure and operate the available Compliance Platform applications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prepare during review

An organization in the `Under Review` state remains available for administrative setup. You can use the review period to prepare the operating structure that will be required after activation.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Create applications" icon="folder-plus">
    Create and configure the applications that the organization plans to operate.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Build the access structure" icon="users-round">
    Add members and define the access structure for the organization and its applications.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Note>
  Applications created during the review period are not operational. An application can be used only after it reaches the `Active` state.
</Note>

## Organization status

| Status | Meaning | Platform access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `Under Review` | The organization has been created and its registration request is awaiting verification. | Administrative setup is available, but applications cannot operate. |
| `Active` | The platform administrator has verified and approved the organization. | Authorized members can use the organization's available applications. |

<Note>
  Creating an account or organization does not activate operational access. The organization must reach the `Active` state first.
</Note>
