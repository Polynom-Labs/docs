---
title: "Bind a deployed mint"
description: "Record a Solana mint and activate the confidential Asset."
openapi: "/openapi/compliance-platform-service.json PATCH /v1/assets/{id}/mint"
playground: "none"
---

Call this method after your backend deploys the Token-2022 confidential mint.
Provide `mintAddress`. You can also provide the deployment transaction
signature and the status reported by your deployment service.

Arcane records the supplied values, sets the Asset to `active`, and attempts to
register the mint for indexing. Use [Get indexer
status](/products/compliance-platform/integration/api-reference/service-api/assets/get-indexer-status)
to confirm registration.
