Connect confidential assets and confidential applications to the Arcane
Compliance Platform. After integration, your team can operate them through the
Arcane interface or through the User API. The Solana SDP integration also uses
a focused Service API for confidential-asset provisioning and indexing.

This section documents the **Compliance Platform** only. The **Privacy Layer**
is a separate Arcane product with its own integration documentation and API
surface.

## Integrate assets or applications

The Compliance Platform supports two integration paths. You can use either
path independently or combine them in one product.

| Integration | Use it for | Main actions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Confidential asset** | Issue a stablecoin, tokenized real-world asset, or another confidential asset. | Connect the asset to Arcane so authorized operators can use Compliance Platform workflows. The Solana SDP flow can provision an Asset and register its mint through the Service API. |
| **Confidential application** | Operate a private payroll product, payment gateway, transfer application, treasury product, or another confidential financial workflow. | Register the application, associate its confidential activity, configure access, and manage cases, risk incidents, reports, and activity records. |

## Choose compatible privacy technology

You can use the Compliance Platform when your confidential application and
assets use a compatible privacy technology stack. Current compatible stacks
include:

- the Arcane Privacy Layer on Stellar and Solana. Both network integrations use
  zero-knowledge (ZK) proofs;
- Solana confidential tokens built with Token-2022 Confidential Transfer;
- Fhenix confidential tokens built with fully homomorphic encryption (FHE).

The privacy technology protects transaction execution and confidential asset
data. The Compliance Platform adds organization access, policy, review,
controlled disclosure, reporting, and activity evidence around that protected
activity.

Compatibility depends on the privacy technology stack, not the product
category. Any confidential financial product can connect when it uses a
compatible stack.

<Info>
  If you provide privacy technology and want to integrate it with Arcane,
  contact [max@arcane.finance](mailto:max@arcane.finance).
</Info>

## Operate through Arcane

Once connected, you can work with confidential assets and applications through
the Compliance Platform interface. The interface gives authorized users access
to organization setup, applications and assets, team permissions, cases,
disclosure workflows, risk incidents, reports, and activity records.

Arcane also exposes two API surfaces:

| API | Use it for | Authentication | Authorization |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **User API** | Build a custom interface or another user-driven workflow with documented, user-authorized Compliance Platform actions. | User access token | The signed-in user's organization membership, permissions, assignments, and approved data scope. |
| **Service API** | Provision a confidential Asset, bind its Solana mint, and read indexer status from a trusted backend. | Organization API key | The organization selected by the key. |
